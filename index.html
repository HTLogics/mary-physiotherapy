MARY PHYSIOTHERAPY CLINIC

Our philosophy at Mary Physiotherapy Clinic is to provide each patient with the best care possible in order for them to reach their goals as quickly as possible.

Our professionals treat each of our clients with one-on-one, quality care, helping them achieve a healthy and full recovery. We utilize therapeutic methods of healing such as traditional physiotherapy, Craniosacral therapy, guided exercise programs, postural correction and kinesiotaping.
